Matt James Visuals
Matt James' works have been exhibited in solo and group exhibitions throughout Colorado including Sizzle and Bang Gallery, The Matchbox, and Infinite Monkey Theorem. In addition, he has commissioned many pieces for private owners as well as a large mural for the U.S. Forest Service. Matt James has been the recipient of numerous awards and honors including a First Place prize for his piece "the Break," by the Pueblo Art Guild. James grew up in southern Colorado where he studied under Dave and Anne McKean and has been living and working in Denver for over 10 years. He has been painting primarily figurative pieces using charcoal and oils for over 15 years.
